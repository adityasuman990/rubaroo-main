DRAMA EVENTS

Halla Bol! - Nukkad Natak  

Halla Bol! is the street play or nukkad natak contest at Rubaroo where we talk of the people, and for the people. The nukkad is a form of expression most freeing and liberating, via which we explore the most pressing social issues that affect the common folk.

Ekalaap - MonoAct

Ekalaap is the Monoact contest at Rubaroo. Monoact, also known as monodrama or solo performance, is a powerful form of artistic expression. Do you have the ability to captivate the audience's attention through your portrayal, with a range of emotions, thoughts, and actions?
